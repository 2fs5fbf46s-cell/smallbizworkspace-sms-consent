        <h2>How Consent (Opt-In) is Collected</h2>

        <p>

          Consumers opt in by providing their phone number inside the SmallBiz Workspace app or

          client portal and completing an explicit consent action (e.g., selecting a checkbox or

          confirmation control) before SMS messaging is enabled.

        </p>

        <p class="muted">

          Consent is collected only for transactional and informational communications related to an

          existing business relationship. No marketing or promotional messaging is sent.

        </p>

      </section>


      <section class="card">

        <h2>Consent Disclosure Shown to Consumers</h2>

        <p>Consumers are presented with clear disclosure language similar to:</p>

        <blockquote>

          “By providing your phone number, you agree to receive SMS notifications from SmallBiz Workspace

          regarding invoices, contracts, appointments, and service updates. Message and data rates may apply.

          Reply STOP to opt out or HELP for support.”

        </blockquote>

      </section>

      <h2>Message Types & Triggers</h2>

      <p>Messages are triggered by actions within the app, including:</p>

      <ul>

        <li>Invoice notifications and payment confirmations</li>

        <li>Contract delivery and signature reminders</li>

        <li>Appointment scheduling, reminders, and rescheduling updates</li>

        <li>Service/job status updates (started, delayed, completed)</li>

      </ul>

      <p class="muted">

        Message frequency varies by consumer activity, typically 1–5 messages per transaction or service event.

      </p>
